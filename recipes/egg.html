Soft Boiled Egg

Quick, Easy and Luscious Soft Boiled Egg

Have you ever wondered how to get restaurant quality Soft boild eggs at home? Wonder no more, I'm here to lay easy your mind. It's as easy as getting water to boil then putting on a lid.

Seriously! All you do is get the water boiling, put the egg in, remove it at just the right time and a few mintues later you have a delicious, soft and runny egg yoke.

Ingredients

  • Pot or Pan
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Heat Source

Steps

  1. Bing water to boil
  2. Place room-temperature egg in boiling water
  3. Boil for 4 minutes
  4. Submerge egg in cold water
