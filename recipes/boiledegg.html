Easy Hard Boiled Eggs!

Hard boiled egss are a staple human diet. They are also one of the easiest meals you can prepare yourself. Follow along for the directions to create this,the oldest meal known to man, based on chicken eggs, that is...

Ingredients

  • Raw egg in unbroken shells
  • Pot large enough to contain the eggs
  • Enough water to cover the eggs

Steps

  1. Put the eggs in the pot
  2. Cover the eggs with water
  3. Put the pot on heat source
  4. When at a rolling boil, cover and remove from heat
  5. Wait at least 15 minutes, then cool the eggs in the coldest water you have access to
