Fancy Instant Ramen Noodles

Time to Jazz Up that Instant Ramen

Instant Ramen isn't just for poor college kids anymore! He is a way to really up your ramen game, and it all stuff you probably have in your kitchen already. If you don't then you need ot seriously invest some Time in the kitchen basics. First, we prep everything. Get a few vegetables to chuck in. I like carrots and potatoes. Cut them up into small pieces, whatever shape you prefer. Next we need some seasoning. I use the season packet included but I mix in some chicken stock and yes, cubes work too. Then I throw in some garlic and ginger. Dump a bit of soy in, dark or light. Taste it! Boil all this together. when the vegetables are done, chuck in the noodles. When those are done, dump it into a bowl. Dress with spring onions and a soft- boiled egg -- or whatever you like, really.

Ingredients

  • Packet of your favorite instant Ramen
  • A few vegetables of your choice to add substance
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Soy sauce of your prefered shade

Steps

  1. Prep your veg, garlic and ginger
  2. Boil water and toss in above ingredients
  3. Add soy and chicken stock to taste
  4. When veg are nearly done, toss in noodles
  5. When noodbels are soft, serve into large bowl
  6. Top with onion and egg if available
